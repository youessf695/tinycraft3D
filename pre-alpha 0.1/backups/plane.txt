كود التجميع للمترجم: 
gcc -m32 main.c src/*.c -o TinyCraft.exe -I./SDL2/include -L./SDL2/lib -lmingw32 -lSDL2main -lSDL2 -lopengl32 -lSDL2_ttf -lm -mwindows

features:
1. flat world.
2. Demolition and construction.
3. Three types of materials ("Grass, Stone, Wood") With the ability to choose between them.
4. It can run and fly.
5. It returns to the surface if it falls into the void.
6. You can save the world and load it again from the "world3d.dat" file.
7. The player's position, material type, and player coordinates (xyz) are written on the screen with the phrase "World saved successfully" in "arial" font.
8. Maximum player height is 250 blocks.
